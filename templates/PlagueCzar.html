Acquired Message

You left that space a changed man. Some could say that your life is on a new vector. Creatures wiggles beneath your skin. Your blood pools due to resistance. Your mind is unfettered by things others call "rationality"

Template

Description: Your body develops deep bruising all over. Your skin seems thin. Others think you sick.

Weakness:

Bad to be Around: You suffer a -2 to persuasion.

Disease Adjacent: Certain magics designed to combat diseases are more effect against you. e.g. Cure Disease now does 2d6 damage against you.

Obvious Symptoms: You are less likely to be allowed into cities.

Powers:

Bad to be Around: You suffer a +2 to intimidate.

Miasma Aura: A thick cloud hangs around you. All of life and death seems to be thrown off. Within one 1" of you (aura) positive and negative energy are less effective. When either heals, they heal for two fewer wounds than normal. When either damages, treat all creatures within your aura as having +2 toughness.

Festering Wounds: When you take a wound from a physical attack, all those within your aura are affected by the Physical Disease Table. Vigor resists.

Dirty Mind: When you take a wound from a mental attack, all those within your aura are affected by the Mental Disease Table. Smarts resists.

Plague Czar Edges

Parasitic Touch

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Novice

Your body hosts entire ecosystems of "friends". When you deal wounds with natural weapons roll on the Parasitic Disease table. An agility check is used to oppose parasitic infections.

Come in Pairs

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Veteran, Parasitic Touch

When you cause someone to roll on the Parasitic Disease table they also roll on the Physical Disease table.

Expanded, Parasitic Diseases

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Veteran, Parasitic Touch, Agility d6

Your bodily biome has developed new niches allowing for more parasites. Add the Parasitic Addon table on to the end of the Parasitic Disease table making it a d12 table.

Expanded, Physical Diseases

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Seasoned, Vigor d6

You have developed a method to keep some disease for competing for resources. Add the Physical Addon table on to the end of the Physical Disease table making it a d12 table.

Expanded, Mental Diseases

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Seasoned, Smarts d6

Your mind has expanded to hold more truths of the universe. Add the Mental Addon table on to the end of the Mental Diseases table making it a d12 table.

Filling in the Gaps

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Novice

You take an inflicted, active disease breaking the target (i.e. curing the disease) to recover from wounds inflicted on you by jamming it into the open sore. If wounds were "mental" must be a mental disease, if physical it must be parasitic.

Cocktail, Confounding

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Veteran

Your diseases do not seem to obey normal vectors. Select four diseases from each disease table you possess. This forms a new table you can use in place of the default table when a Plague Czar ability is triggered. The table takes 3 time periods to retrain. With only Plague Czar this will be 8 (Physical, Mental) or 12 (Physical, Mental, Parasitic). If it would result in 16 select only 12 with a max of 4 from one table, if it would result in more than 20 but less than 100 limit it to 20 with a max of 4 from each table, if it is more than 100 limit it to 100 with a max of 4 from each table.

Cocktail, Specialized

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Heroic, Confounding Cocktail

You have refined your diseases down to a select few. Select 2 from each your Plague Czar disease tables, ignoring Confounding Cocktail, to construct a new table. Will result in a d4 or d6 table. Re-specializing the table takes 3 time periods. Either this or Confounding Cocktail can be used with any Plague Czar disease trigger.

Air, Stale

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Novice

Your miasma slowly expands when you stand still. For each round you stand still expand your aura another inch to a maximum of +3 additional inches.

Air, Contagion

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Seasoned

Your miasma is a thick cloud that wafts from you. Miasma aura is 2".

Air, Toxic

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Heroic, Contagion Air

The air around is so toxic trees begin to wilt. Miasma aura is now 4".

Air, Deadly

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Legendary, Toxic Air

Nothing dare get too close for death hangs around you. Miasma aura is 6". In addition once per time period you can force someone in your aura to roll on either the Mental Diseases or Physical Disease table without a triggering event.

Trigger Warning

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Legendary

Your presence causes the disease to fester at unprecedented rates. Once per session, you target someone infected with one of your diseases inside of your aura and cause the disease to advance a stage. e.g. If incubating, 1st stage starts immediately.

Trigger Warning, Episodic

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Legendary, Trigger Warning

You can now trigger stage advancement 3 times per session.

Disease Capture

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Novice, Heal d10

Your perception and understanding of disease allows you to capture diseases that try to afflict you. Instead of suffering effects of a disease you store it for later use. You may only store one disease at a time this way. When you would roll for a disease of that type you may instead chose to use the captured disease. If using the captured disease the slot is freed and the captured disease can not be used again.

Disease Incubator

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Veteran, Disease Capture, Heal d12

When afflicting a captured disease it has already passed its incubation period. 1st stage effects begin immediately.

Petri Dish

Requirements: Acquired Race: Plague Czar, Veteran, Disease Capture

You may capture an additional disease. This can be taken again at heroic and legendary.
