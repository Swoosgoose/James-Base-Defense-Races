Requirements:

Must free three powerful beings who have been sealed for at least 5 years.

Acquired Message

You feel a weight lift from your body. The trial is over and you know you passed. Who exactly was that you just freed? Well not that it is your concern now. You turn around to leave...

Template

Description: You seem to have a sheen to you. Others may say you look greasy or slimy but they are just jealous of your good vibes.

Weakness:

No One Should be Bound: You take a -2 on any action to restrict other characters movements and a -1 to all actions if you passively allow others to bind them. This stacks with itself.

Powers:

No One Should be Bound: You gain a +2 to resist any action to restrict your movements. Gain +2 to direct actions used to free others.

Here lies: You have a nth sense for powerful sealed/bound beings. You know the cardinal direction to a powerful bound being. Once freed another is chose at random.

Seal Breaker Edges

Just Passing Through

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Novice

Doors were meant to open and for you they do. When you attempt to open a door make a spirit check. On success, non-magically locked or jammed doors will open as if they were perfectly functional, unlocked doors. On a raise, the door is unlocked/unblocked for you only (you may hold the door) and no evidence remains that it has been breached.

No Door Blocks Me

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Veteran, Just Passing Through

Doors are installed to keep out others. When you open a door make a spirit check. If the door is magically sealed, it is either opposed or you suffer a -1 per rank of sealing spell (GM selects). On a raise, the door can not be locked/blocked the same way again for five time periods.

Unbindable

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Heroic

It is hard to hold you in one place against your will. You can not be targeted by spells or magical effects that restrict their movement, including binding, grappling, paralysis, petrification and pinning, as well as spells like Entangle, Lethargy, Sleep, Stasis and Web.

Know Your Exits

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Novice

When you walk into a room you are able to pick out all the doors. Non-magically hidden doors do not require a notice. Magically hidden doors require only a notice check.

Escape Artist

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Seasoned, Know Your Exits

Your uncanny ability to find exitse to a room improves. You can find any exite that is considered part of the room just like you find doors. You must be able to fit through the exite to detect it

Making Your Own Exit

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Veteran, Escape Artist

You can feel the "flow" of space and use that to find exitse in walls. These spaces are unstable and dangerous to be in for extended periods of time. Once per time period you may use this to move through a wall. This space is at least as long as the walls width. If you end a round in this space, bad things may happen. Some magic may prevent this ability, but the default magical nature does not. Some walls may not have paths throughpt them. You can make a notice to verify a path exists.

Finding Your Way Out

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Heroic, Making Your Own Exit

You are able to spot more "gaps". You can now see pathways that are within 1" of the room you are in. You can use Making Your Own Exit twice per time period. You may expend two uses of Making Your Own Exit to shorten the distance of the path by 1".

Taking the Road I Know

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Finding Your Way Out

Most walls appear to be more hole than wall. Very few magics will stop Making Your Own Exit. You can use Making Your Own Exit three times per time period. You may expend a third uses of Making Your Own Exit to shorten a path by 2" more (3" [6 meters] total).

Read the Room

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Seasoned

Not everyone wants to be freed, as odd as that may seem. You get a +2 to streetwise checks to determine if an entity would be grateful, neutral, or resentful at being "freed".

Let Your Imagination Run Wild

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Novice

Creatures roam the unconscious world. However due to their nature they are unable to enter this world. That is just so sad. You can help these unfortunate souls. Once per time period you can free a legendary creature from "your" unconscious. It appears randomly within 3" of you and is hostile to everything. It disappears after 10 rounds. If summoning magic is blocked this will not work. However if part of the 3" is outside of a no summoning space this will still activate. If the creature moves into the space after it does not disappear.

Reigning in Your Imagination

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Read the Room, Let Your Imagination Run Wild

You are better at selecting imaginary creatures that will not attack you. Roll a smarts roll, on success you summon a creature who will not attack you but may not (d100) attack anyone either. On a raise, you summon a creature who will align with you. On a double raise, you summon a creature who not only aligns with you but also has characteristics you desired.

Hidden Keys

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Seasoned

A presence guides you to mechanism involved in the sealing/binding/jailing. It does not help determine if it is a "key" or "lock" nor does it help with the "use" but it does guide you to it.

Escape Guidance

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Veteran, Hidden Keys

The same presence that helps you find keys also warns you of guards/pursuers around blind corners allowing a +2 to stealth and perception checks.

Long Forgotten Knowledge

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Heroic, Hidden Keys

You have an innate understanding of bindings and unbindings. When reading unknown text you can you can clearly pick out passages on sealing and unsealing though the rest remains unreadable. In addition you have a +2 Knowledge Arcana and Enchanting rolls to figure out how to disable magical bindings.

Impulsive Instigator

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Veteran

People are so tied down due to their inhibitions but with a bit of a nudge you can fix that. Once per time period, roll persuasion, taunt or spirit against the targets spirit or smarts. On success you cause them to take an impulsive action that they were resisting.

A Matter of Control

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Impulsive Instigator

People are so free when they talk to you. Gain a +2 on Impulsive Instigator. In addition, on a raise you can influence action results of Impulsive Instigator.

Free From the Shackles of Time

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Heroic

You imagine the best possible way to spring someone from the slammer. Once per session, while still alive you may restore to the start of a prison break pb. Time periods used on the attempt still passed. However everything involved in the escape seems to reset. You are back where you started. Other characters have no recollection of your break attempt. Doors relock, traps reset, alarms are lowered, people "revive" for free.

Letting the Heat Out

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Free From the Shackles of Time

Doors you imagined to be locked turn out to be unlocked allowing you or others to get through them without keys or checks. After activating Free From the Shackles of Time the doors do not relock, though they do return to their state (closed/ajar/open).

Triggering Flashbacks

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Free From the Shackles of Time

Whether it is dumb luck or something else, traps you imagined are often sprung or disarmed. Traps do not reset after activating Free From the Shackles of Time.

Return by Death

Requirements: Acquired Race: Seal Breaker, Legendary, Free From the Shackles of Time

Death is only the end if you let it be. You may activate Free From the Shackles of Time after you die. The prison break requirement stands.
