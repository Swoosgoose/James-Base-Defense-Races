Acquired Message

You don't look very different. Oh! That is about to change though! The book contained ways to expand your existence. You can now link your existence to more bones than you originally had.

Template

Description: Those who can see your mind see extra goopy bits reaching about. Otherwise you are a "normal" skeleton... so far.

Weakness:

Multi-form: Spells, mental effects, consciousness, and other effects appear to originate from the controlled body(s). All required spell components (verbal, material, somatic) must originate from one body.

Multi-control: Swapping control between bodies is difficult. Take -2 multi-action penalty for actions performed after a swap. Stacks with other multi-action penalties and itself.

Resurrections: Only one bone set (body) will be resurrected. Unused sets are consumed as if they were your only body. Additional body(s) must be manually regathered.

Powers:

Multi-form: You possess an extra set of bones (body). If an intact set is within smarts range1 when a controlled body "dies" your consciousness clings to the intact set. Bones you add to yourself take on your properties (i.e. they possess your physical stats).

Multi-control: If your bodies are within smarts range1 you can swap control between them as free actions on your turn.

Travel Mode: You can pack an additional bone set in controlled bodies.

Resurrections: Guildstone resurrections need at least 51% of the most complete set of bones (body) for "distance" purposes.

Horde Lich Edges

Bones, Dem

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Novice

You have expanded your minds capacity for bones. You can now hook three bodies worth of bones to your mind. Bone gathering is still required.

Bones, Dem Dry

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Veteran, Dem Bones

You make your bones unnaturally dry which lessens the burden on your mind. You can now hook four bodies worth of bones to your mind. Each weighs half your original weight and you take +4 from bludgeoning weapons and fire. Bone gathering is still required.

Action at a Distance

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Novice

Your body spasms almost as if it were controlled by someone else. You can get one of your extra bone sets within smarts range1 to perform an action without transferring control. These still take from your action pool.

Action at a Distance, Spooky

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Seasoned, Action at a Distance

Your body's spasms are so advanced they can accomplish relatively complex tasks. You can get one of your extra bone sets within smarts range1 to perform two actions without transferring control. These still take from your action pool.

Coordination, Puppet Show

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Veteran, Smarts d8

Like a puppet master you control multiple bodies at once. Multiple bodies can act at once. Each body can move and use up to two actions. Take a multi-action penalty for each body you control in one round. Additional actions bodies take past the first affect multi-action penalties for all bodies. When actively controlled bodies are outside of smarts range1 of all other controlled bodies double multi-action penalties. Upon death of an actively controlled body, a -1 is applied to future rolls for the next 1d4 rounds unless you swap to an inactive body within smarts range1. All other active bodies stay active.

Coordination, Ringleader

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Heroic, Puppet Show Coordination, Smarts d10

It has become easier to coordinate actions like a troupe. You can coordinate tasks more efficiently. Additional actions taken by individual bodies no longer affect multi-action penalties of every other body, only itself.

Coordination, Head Ringer

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Legendary, Ringleader Coordination, Smarts d12, Agility d6

Each ring runs smoothly with only occasional hiccups. Your multi-action penalties for controlling multiple bodies is reduced to one half. (e.g. Two bodies normally incur a -4 penalty, but for you it is now -2)

Coordination, Perfect

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Legendary, Head Ringer Coordination, Smarts d12+2, Agility d10

Your bodies are perfectly in sync and few commands are sent to the wrong body. Your multi-action penalties for controlling multiple bodies is reduced to one fourth. (e.g. Two bodies normally incur a -4 penalty, but for you it is now -1)

Contortions, Body

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Novice, Smarts d6, Agility d6

By cannibalizing one of your bodies for bones you can add controllable extra limbs to a body. Use one set of bones (regatherable) to gain one of the following on a body:

Extra legs: Body gains 1 die step to agility and +2 pace1.

Extra arms: Body gains 1 die step to strength and +1/4 fighting die parry.

Only one body may have body contortions at a time. Only one body contortion can be applied to a body at a time. You may pack either extra body's bones or the body contortions' bones in a controlled body. Contorted bodies can't be packed in other bodies.

Contortions, Questionable

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Seasoned, Body Contortions, Smarts d8, Agility d8

Messing around you have discovered that your body need not conform to normal rules. You may extend legs or arms, or place a head inside a ribcage. These count as body contortions.

Extended legs: Body loses 1 die step from agility but gains +2 pace1. (Stacks with extra legs)

Extended arms: Body gains +11 reach.

Head in a Ribcage: Body takes -2 to sight & sound based perception. Head's toughness increases by 2 and called shots suffer an additional -2.

Contortions, Creative

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Legendary, Questionable Contortions, Smarts d12, Agility d10, Vigor d8

Your mind can accommodate crazy anatomy changes. Extra heads, feet, and rib cages are no issue. In addition to body contortions available from feats you can propose new ones to your GM. GM has final say.

Multi-heads: You have extended awareness of your surroundings. Gain +1 gang up bonus and all-around vision. May be applied to a single body more than once (if slots available), but only increases gang up bonus.

Double Palmed Hands: Increase Agility and a crafting skill one die step. May be applied to a single body more than once (if slots available), but only increases a crafting skill one die step.

Spine arms: Gain a die step to fighting.

Contortions, Rubber Bone

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Seasoned, Body Contortions, Vigor d6

Your bones are malleable allowing for uncommon configurations. You may apply two body contortions to a body at a time.

Contortions, Body Weave

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Veteran, Rubber Bone Contortions, Vigor d8

Your bones are supple enough to be weaved together. You may apply three body contortions to a body at a time. Two contortion slots can be used to carry an un-contorted body. Three contortion slots allow you to carry a fully "contorted" body.

Contortions, Skeletal Pretzel

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Heroic, Body Weave Contortions, Vigor d10

Your bones contort almost as if made of cartilage. You may apply four body contortions to a body at a time.

Bodies of Wonder

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Veteran, Body Contortions, Agility d8

You found someone who looks like you... YOU! Two bodies can have body contortions applied.

Freak Show

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Heroic, Bodies of Wonder, Agility d10

People would probably pay to see you if it were not so dangerous. Three bodies can have body contortions applied.

Whole Circus

Requirements: Acquired Race: Horde Lich, Legendary, Freak Show, Agility d10

All of you appear to be freaks. Four bodies can have body contortions applied.

1) Savage World Inches
