Acquired Message

You went through the device and came out different. Things seem to be in the right places... if not a little stringy(?). While all is well that ends well.

Template

Description: Strings droop from your fingertips. What they are made of is anybody's guess.

Weakness:

Severed Connection: When something is freed from your control you take back shock. -2 all actions for the next round. This stacks if multiple connections are cut.

Inversed Control: If a person resisting your control action gets a raise against you, they control a single action of yours instead.

Fragile String: Strings, like finger nails or hair, are apart of you but not capable of inflicting wounds if damaged. Unfortunately that means they are not subject to your resistances or immunities and are easily severed by slashing weapons and broken by fire. If a slashing weapon or fire attack connects with the string it is severed.

Powers:

Remote control (Person): You extend your finger strings into a target within 2" of your fingers. You may attempt force the victim to take an action. Make a stealth or agility roll opposed by the targets agility, fighting, or strength. On a raise the target suffers a -2 to oppose future controlled action until the string is cut or you relinquish control. You may only control one person or object at a time.

Remote control (Object): You extend your finger strings onto an object within 2" of your fingers. If you normally be able to throw the object you may move it with your strings. As an action you can fling the object in a straight line 1" to 5". If this collides with anything treat it as a throw attack with half strength. You may only control one person or object at a time.

Puppet Master Edges

Demanding Master

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Heroic

Resisting your strings is exhausting. When you force a puppet to take an action it counts against their multi-action penalty for the round.

Puppets, Conversing

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Seasoned, Agility d6

To be a real master you need more than one puppet and you have figured out how to do that. You can now control another puppet (object or person) at the same time.

Puppets Scene

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Heroic, Conversing Puppets, Agility d8

The complexity or your performance has risen. You can control an additional puppet.

Puppet Show

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Legendary, Puppets Scene, Agility d10

You can put on a real performance. You can control an additional puppet and an additional object.

Articulation, Better

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Novice

You may attach another string to a "puppet" allowing for better control. The victim suffer a -1 to resist and you gain a +1 to control actions.

Articulation, Full

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, Better Articulation

You attach a third string to a "puppet" allowing for better control. The victim suffers a -2 to resist and you gain a +2 to control actions.

Jerky Movements

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Novice

When throwing a object with your string you jerk it about. Gain a +1 to the throwing roll, path no longer needs to be a straight line but still must be limited to 1" to 5".

Caught Line

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Novice

After controlling a puppet, you may use a free action to throw off one of their attacks by pulling the line taut. They take a -1 to fighting or throwing rolls. -2 to spellcasting and shooting rolls.

Magic Fingers

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Seasoned

You are skilled at gaining control of puppets. +1 to remote control rolls.

Master Manipulator

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, Magic Fingers

Your threads know how to weave their way through a puppet's body. +2 to remote control rolls. You may now throw objects you would normally only be able to lift.

Master's Will

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Heroic, Master Manipulator

You have refined your craft to avoid the puppets getting the best of you. Now takes a double raise to for a puppet to gain control.

String, Silk

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Seasoned

Your strings are less vulnerable to being cut. It has a toughness of 6 now. Fire still only needs to connect to destroy the string.

String, Steel

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, Silk String

Your string is stronger than skin. Toughness is now an 8. Fire needs to deal 6 damage to destroy it.

String, Cutting

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, Steel String

Your skill allows you to use your string as a weapon. Melee attack with reach of "control" distance that deals 2d6 slashing damage. It has a +1 to hit when called shots on limbs.

String, Titanium

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Herioc, Steel String

Despite its width your string is shockingly hard to cut. Toughness is now an 12. Fire needs to deal 8 damage to destroy it.

String, Adamantium

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Legendary, Titanium String

You string has a silver-ish appearance and can slice through others easily. Toughness is now an 14. Fire needs to deal 10 damage to destroy it. In addition your "Cutting String" counts as adamantium for purposes of damage.

More to it

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Novice

Your string can extend deceptively far. You can now control people up to 3" away. Objects can be thrown up to 8" now.

Whole Nine Yards

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, More to it

You can pull out more string than expected. You can now control people up to 4" away. Objects can be thrown up to 10" now.

You're in Range

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Legendary, Whole Nine Yards

Your string is practically a ranged weapon. You can now control people up to 5" away. Objects can be thrown up to 12" now.

Emergency Sutures

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Novice, Heal d4

You can use your string to close a wound. Ignore a wound penalty.

All Tied Together

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Seasoned, Emergency Sutures

You sever a string to help an ally. You can apply "Emergency Sutures" to team mates at the cost of one controllable puppet.

Reattachable Arm

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Veteran, Emergency Sutures, Heal d6

You can close more serious wounds with your string. Ignore two wound penalties.

Nothing but Net

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Heroic, Reattach Arm, Heal d8

You can reassemble severely mangled bodies. Ignore up to five wound penalties.

All Tied Up

Requirements: Acquired Race: Puppet Master, Legendary, Nothing but Net, All Tied Together

Your sutures can dissolve in a standard body. You can use your sutures to heal a wound instead of ignoring the penalties. This consumes one puppet's worth of control for five time periods.
