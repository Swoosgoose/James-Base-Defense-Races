Heroic, Davy Jones’ Locker

Acquired Message

The call of the sea was so alluring. One day while exploring its depths it seemed to speak and you chose to further your bond.

Template

Description: You've gained some fishy features. Fine! Aquatic. What's the difference!?!

Weakness:

Water Bound: Suffer a -1 to all actions outside of the water. -2 if in a dry biome like Desert or active Volcano.

Powers:

Dingy: To help you move about a small ship was provided to you. Can fit you, another person, and a little booty. You can summon it to any body of water with enough surface area for it regardless of depth.

Strange Anatomy: Gain 1 to 3 features of aquatic cratures, GM approves. Pre-approved examples:

Claw: Gain a Natural Crushing attack (d6)

Lobster Tail: Retreat without provoking an attack 3"

Suckered Arms: 3" Climb speed outside of Water

Tentacle Beard: +2 to sleight of hand checks with small items

Angler Fish Lure: As a free action you can turn on the bioluminescence of the lure. Grants +2 notice and -2 stealth.

Davy Jones Edges

Sprung a Leak

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Actually Fluid Body, Agility d12

Physical obstacles are rarely impediments. You can pass through any structure that water can trickle through without impacting your movement.

Kracken, Release the

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Greater Animal Friend

The sea sends you a champion. Make a heroic extra companion based on an aquatic creature. This creature can be summoned anywhere the surface area of the water can fit it regardless of depth. If it is killed a new identical one will take its place in 2 weeks.

Kracken, Legendary

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Release the Kracken

Your champion has gained skills above others of his ilk. Upgrade the creature to a legendary extra and give it an extra wound capacity for free.

Kracken, Legends of the

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Release the Kracken

Your champion affects the world more than before. The creature is now a wild card.

Lugger

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones

You dingy gets larger and sprouts sails. You may change the ship (e.g. back to the dingy) by spending 3 time periods to refocus it.

Capacity10 medium creatures and 500 lbs of cargo OR 2000 lbs of cargo plus crew (2)
Acceleration/Top Speed (TS)2/10 (8 TS at max Capacity)
Manning Requirements1 or Ship Control
Toughness10

Cutter

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Lugger

Your ship's complexity increases while it loses some weight making it more fragile.

Capacity8 medium creatures and 600 lbs of cargo OR 1200 lbs of cargo plus crew (3)
Acceleration/Top Speed (TS)4/16
Manning Requirements3 or Ship Control
Toughness8

Brigantine

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Cutter

The ship grows once more adding rows for cannons and a dingy hanging off the side. You do not need to refocus to use or summon the dingy.

Capacity25 medium creatures and 2000 lbs of cargo OR 5000 lbs of cargo plus crew (8)
Acceleration/Top Speed (TS)2/8
Manning Requirements5 or Ship Control
Toughness12 (Heavy Armor)
Cannon Capacity4

Galleon

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Brigantine

The ship grows larger adding a deck for cannons, while the dingy still hangs. Do not need to refocus for dingy.

Capacity100 medium creatures and 2000 lbs of cargo OR 20,000 lbs of cargo plus crew (20)
Acceleration/Top Speed (TS)2/6
Manning Requirements12 or Ship Control
Toughness20 (Heavy Armor)
Cannon Capacity24

Currents, Shipping

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Currents Vortex

Your ship is faster than others of its type. Increase its Top Speed by 2".

Navigable Waters

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Currents Vortex

(W) Rough waters and storms do not affect your ship. Maneuvering rolls are not needed to pilot the ship.

Turn her About

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Navigable Waters

(W) Your ship responds to your thoughts. While on board your ship you do not need a crew to man the ship in order to move it.

Fire on the Starboard Side

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Epic, Turn her About, Part of the Ship, Brigantine

The ocean provides two cannons (W) with bottomless ammo. Only crew members from Part of the Ship may operate the cannons with endless ammo supplied by the sea. Part of the Ship crew members have a shooting of d12 and the cannons do 4d12 force heavy weapon damage. For those not Part of the Ship, they can use the cannons as normal cannons that deal 2d12 heavy weapon damage.

Dive Dive Dive

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Turn her About

(W) While you are controlling the ship you may move vertically in the water. It must be deep enough to submerge. Submerging floods the ship potentially drowning crew members and knocking improperly secured cargo off. Surfacing causes water to poor out of the ship potentially dragging cargo and crew with it.

Batten Down the Hatch

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Dive Dive Dive

You can confer part of the sea's blessings to others. When using Dive Dive Dive you can choose cargo and crew to avoid negative effects as long as they stay on board (e.g. no drowning, forced movement, etc)

Connected Waters

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Dive Dive Dive

You may submerge the ship and instantly reemerge it anywhere you are familiar with that has water surface area large enough for the ship you control. NOTE: To get the ship out of too shallow waters you will need to summon it to you somewhere else.

Part of the Ship

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones

You can recruit a person who died at sea to man your ship. They may not leave the ship. They are considered corporeal undead but do not take up space on the ship.

Part of the Crew

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Part of the Ship

You may recruit two more (3) people who died at sea.

Skeleton Crew

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Part of the Crew, Brigantine

You may recruit four more (7) people who died at sea.

Only the Damned

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Skeleton Crew, Galleon

You may recruit five more (12) people who died at sea.

Sea's Bounty

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Davy Jones' Locker

The sea provides and none know that better than you. When you sink another ship, you gain 10% of its cargo.

Ocean's Bounty

Requirements: Acquired Race: Davy Jones, Legendary, Sea's Bounty

The ocean spoils you so. When you sink another ship, you gain 20% of its cargo.
