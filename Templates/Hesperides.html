Acquired Message

The seed finally sprouted. A small tree has begun to grow and already bears fruit, a single golden apple. You feel compelled to eat it, which puts you in a deep slumber. Upon waking you find yourself with some dazzling purple, orange, and/or yellow highlights.

Template

Description: Your eyes, hair, and/or skin color have hints, highlights, and/or outright change to yellow, orange, and/or purple

Weakness:

Fruits of Love: While the tree does not bare any fruit suffer a -2 to all actions.

In Need of Nurturing: You or the Dragon must be within 2" of the tree at all times or suffer as if the tree bore no fruit. If there are no fruits on the tree, double the penalties.

Powers:

Dragon: You and the dragon are linked, though you are unsure how. It can take actions for you, you may cast spells through it, and if it is close to the tree you are considered close to the tree. If the dragon dies a new one appears in 5 time periods.

Enchanting: Those who may find you attractive find you irresistible. Gain a +2 charisma.

Golden Apple: Picked apples must be consumed immediately or spoil. A new apple grows every 4 time periods if there is an empty Golden Apple slot. Things marked with (E) are effects of the apple. One effect must be chosen when the apple is consumed.

(E) +2 on next spellcasting

(E) +2 on next heal roll

(E) +2 on next knowledge roll

Hesperides Edges

Baring Fruit

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Novice

The tree has grown a bit. The tree can hold two golden apples at once.

Ripe for the Picking

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Seasoned, Baring Fruit

The tree is now about half the size of a standard apple tree. The tree can hold three golden apples at once.

Bountiful Harvest

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Heroic, Ripe for the Picking

The tree is fully grown. The Tree can hold up to five Golden Apples at once.

Eternal Beauty

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Veteran, Knowledge Nature d8

The fruit of the tree does not age when you are near by. You may pick a Golden Apple. As long as it is near you or the dragon it will not spoil and does not count against the trees limit. Only one can be preserved at a time.

Fruit of Knowledge

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Seasoned, Smarts d6

(E) Eating the fruit helps one concentrate. It grants the consumer a d6 in any knowledge for the rest of the time period.

Core of Knowledge

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Heroic, Fruit of Knowledge, Smarts d8

(E) The fruit sharpens the intellect. Eating the fruit grants the consumer a d10 in any knowledge for the first check and a d6 for the rest of the time period.

Flash of Brilliance

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Legendary, Core of Knowledge, Smarts d10

(E) The fruit causes everything to fall into place. Eating the fruit grants the consumer a d12+2 in any knowledge for the first check and a d10 for the rest of the time period.

Tastes Like Home

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Novice

(E) When you eat a Golden Apple you can be brought back to the tree. This affect only works for you.

Sweeter the Apple

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Novice

(E) When the fruit is consumed power surges through the consumer. The consumer regains a spell slot of the highest level you can cast. If they do not possess an expended spell slot of that level then recover one of the lower spell slots.

An Apple a Day

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Veteran, Heal d6

(E) The apple tastes bitter, but it is good for you. Roll a vigor check to keep the apple down (wounds do not apply). Heal one wound.

Good for your Health

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Legendary, An Apple a Day

(E) The apple almost tastes rancid. Roll a vigor at a -4 to keep the apple down (wounds do not apply). Heal three wounds.

Complex Flavor Profile

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Heroic

There is a depth to the apples flavor that you had not picked up on until now. Two effects may be chosen when consuming a Golden Apple.

Trifecta

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Legendary, Complex Flavor Profile

The apple seems perfect. Three effects may be chosen when consuming a Golden Apple.

Dragon, Watchful

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Seasoned

The dragon has grown and may be able to guard the tree on its own. Create a medium (size 0) veteran extra for the dragon's stat block. Gain a free breath weapon of fire or poison.

Dragon, Guardian

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Heroic, Watchful Dragon

The dragon has grown larger. Create a large (size 1) legendary extra to serve as the dragon's stat block. Gain free flight and a breath weapon of fire or poison.

Dragon, Untamed

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Legendary, Watchful Dragon

The dragon is a master of its fate. The dragon becomes a wild card and can take an additional wound.

Dragon's Breath

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Novice

Your link to the dragon seems stronger. You can use the dragon's breath weapon as an action.

Wandering the Garden

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Novice, Survival d4

You feel as if the tree trusts you can make it in time. You and the dragon can now be up to 4" away from the tree without suffering from the Fruits of Love.

Over the Hill

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Veteran, Wandering the Garden, Survival d8

The tree's trust in you has grown. You and the dragon can now be up to 8" away from the tree without suffering from the Fruits of Love.

Through the Woods

Requirements: Acquired Race: Hesperides, Legendary, Over the Hill, Survival d12

The tree's trust is a little worrying. Is it complacent? You and the dragon can now be up to 32" away from the tree without suffering from the Fruits of Love.
